At Mofa Security Services Ltd, we provide professional Manned Guarding Services tailored to meet the unique security needs of our clients. Our team of well-trained, licensed, and uniformed guards is deployed to various locations including residential estates, commercial buildings, construction sites, schools, hospitals, and industrial facilities. Event Security Management

At Mofa Security Services Ltd, we offer reliable and comprehensive Event Security Management services to ensure the safety and smooth operation of all types of events. Whether it's a corporate gathering, concert, private party, sporting event, political rally, or public celebration, our experienced security personnel are trained to manage crowds, control access points, monitor for suspicious activity, and respond swiftly to any incidents. Mobile Patrol Services

×

At Mofa Security Services Ltd, we provide efficient and proactive Mobile Patrol Services to safeguard properties that require flexible and dynamic security coverage. Our marked patrol vehicles and trained security officers conduct scheduled and random patrols across residential areas, business premises, industrial parks, schools, and other sensitive locations. These patrols serve as a visible deterrent to criminal activity and allow for quick detection of any unusual or unauthorized behavior. Our officers are equipped to respond to alarms, perform perimeter checks, and ensure that all access points remain secure. Whether it's day or night, our mobile patrol teams deliver consistent, responsive protection for clients who need peace of mind without the need for a full-time guard on-site.

Guard Dog & Handler Services

At Mofa Security Services Ltd, we offer specialized Guard Dog & Handler Services to provide an added layer of protection for your property and assets. Our highly trained security dogs, accompanied by professional handlers, are deployed to deter and detect potential intruders, making them ideal for securing large estates, warehouses, industrial sites, farms, and construction areas.
